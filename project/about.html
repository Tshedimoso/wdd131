About ByTheCurator

By The Curator is a sole focus magazine for creatives, focusing on fashion, music, and arts. We take all creatives and put them in one space to showcase their work and talents. By The Curator is more of a mixed media outlet. We believe in the importance of creating a culture where creatives (curators) can express themselves and relay their thoughts and opinions without having to do their creative work. A creative hub where they can relate to their own ideas and connect to and with their audience, not only that but connect with other artists which could spring up new collaborations. This is By The Curator, welcome.
