Site Name

ByTheCurator

ByTheCurator is a name that represents more of a mixed media outlet.It focuses on the importance of creating a culture where creatives (curators) can express themselves and relay their thoughts and opinions without having to do their creative work. A creative hub where they can relate to their own ideas and connect to and with their audience, not only that but connect with other artists which could spring up new collaborations.

Site Purpose

The site's purpose is to promote artists, curate and present artistic content, support creative visibility, and foster engagement between artists and audiences.

Scenarios

  • “I am an emerging artist — how can I submit my work to be featured on ByTheCurator?

  • Where can I explore the latest curated collections and read articles about featured artists?
